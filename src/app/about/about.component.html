How does the Chat Trinity bot work?

Based on ChatGPT's language processing model, the Chat Trinity bot interrogates data from the Trinity College London website www.trinitycollege.com and a number of syllabus, regulation, and guidance documents to auto-generate answers to your questions. If the answer is vague or unhelpful; we recommend asking a more specific question. Over time the quality of answers will improve as the technology learns from your questions.

There is of course no substitute for human interaction! To find Trinity contacts visit your local country page on the Trinity website - https://www.trinitycollege.com/local-trinity
